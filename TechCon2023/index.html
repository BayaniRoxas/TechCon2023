Welcome to TechCon 2023!

Overview

Welcome to TechCon 2023 where the future of technology unfolds in an electrifying convergence of minds, ideas, and innovation. This leading event vows to show off the unparalleled exploration of the latest break throughs, research developments, technolgical advancements, and much more.

Venue details

TechCon 2023 will occur accross two separate campuses at the University of Leicester. Robotics, computer science, and electronics will take place at Sir bob burgess building (LE2 7TF). Whilst medical and biological advancements will take place at percy gee (LE1 7RH)

Dates / Times

Overall opening/closing: 20.11.23 to 23.11.23 from 0800 to 2000
