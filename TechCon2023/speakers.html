More on Bill Gates

Bill Gates, born on October 28, 1955, co-founded Microsoft and is a prominent philanthropist. His influence extends from shaping the tech industry to addressing global issues through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, focusing on health and education.

More on Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg, born on May 14, 1984, is the co-founder and CEO of Facebook, a social media platform that revolutionized online connectivity. Zuckerberg has played a central role in shaping the digital landscape. Beyond Facebook, he has been involved in various philanthropic initiatives, including the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative.

More on Slavoj zizek

Slavoj Žižek, born March 21, 1949, is a Slovenian philosopher and cultural critic known for his provocative work blending critical theory, Marxism, and psychoanalysis. His unique perspective and prolific writings have positioned him as a leading figure in discussions on ideology, politics, and society.
